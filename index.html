Homemade Love

Our Heritage

Bruneian Flavours, Crafted in Our Kitchen.

Ray Ha Savoury started with a simple passion: bringing the rich, comforting taste of homemade savory snacks to our community in Brunei-Muara. What began as a family favorite quickly grew into a home-based business dedicated to quality.

Every wrap and popia is handmade daily using premium ingredients. We believe in transparency and tradition, which is why our products are strictly Halal and free from artificial preservatives. From our table to yours, we bring you "The Goodness" in every bite.
